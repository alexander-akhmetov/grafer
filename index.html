Grafer privacy policy

Last updated: March 31, 2015

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of your health data as provided through the iOS Health app and API (“Health Data”).

By using this app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information collection

Your health data will NOT be sent to any external server. Health data will be accessed locally through Apple’s API and cached by the app.

We will not share your Health Data with anyone.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

We will not change this agreement to make other significant changes without your consent.

Contacts

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
